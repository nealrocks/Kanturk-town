Kanturk Rugby Club

Founded in 1927 Kanturk RFC is a vibrant club with growing numbers each year. There are mini, youth and adult playing teams. New members and players are always welcome!! For more info please go to our webpage https://www.kanturkrugby.com




Kanturk Men's Shed Group

The Men's Shed is a place for men of all ages to meet up with other men in a friendly enviroment. The idea of Mens' Shed is to take part in activities, primarily done for projects in Kanturk town. For more information please go to our facebook page facebook.com/kanturk.mensshed.5
