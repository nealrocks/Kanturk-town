Kanturk (Irish: Ceann Toirc, meaning 'boar's head') is a town in the north west of County Cork, Ireland. It is situated approx 5 to 10miles outside Mallow Town County Cork and in the Kerry direction. The last census of the town population was taken in 2016 which was 2,350.
