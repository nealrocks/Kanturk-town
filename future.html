New Primary School

Currently at the moment there is a new Boy's Primary School been built in the town and it is hoping to be open in 2022. For more information please visit our webpage https://www.kanturkbns.com/

New Relief Road

Currently there is a new Relief Road been built in the Town and will link the Mill Road to Strand street and expected to be completed in 2022. For more information please visit https://www.corkcoco.ie/en/news/kanturk-gain-bike-friendly-link-road

New Aldi Supermarket

There is a new Aldi supermarket coming to Kanturk and work is expected to open its doors in early 2022. For more information please visit https://www.shelflife.ie/aldi-announces-new-supermarket-in-kanturk-co-cork/

State Of The Art Astroturf Pitch

Kanturk are currently fundraising for a state of the art Astroturf pitch hoping to have it completed in 2022. For more information please visit our webpage at https://www.kanturkastroturf.ie/
